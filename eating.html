Zero Waste Eating



These are ways you can implement zero waste into your eating habits



- Compost all of your food scraps like egg shells and vegetables


- Drink from metal or glass straws instead of plastic straws

Buy utensils

- Use reusable water bottles instsead of plastic bottles


- Refrain from eating out unless restaurant


- Meal prep for working rather than buying something from the store


- Use reusable utensils instead of plastic when eating out at places that don't use dishes
