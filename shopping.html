Zero Waste Shopping



These are all ideas on how to shop zero waste or implement some zero waste ideas into your shopping.



- Refrain from buying processed junk food with lots of wrappers


- Put fruits and vegetables in reusable produce bags when shopping rather than plastic


- Prefer to buy food items in carboard bozes rather than plastic bags

(example: pasta)


- Buy pastas, beans, and grains from bulk bins


- Use reusable bags when shopping for groceries
